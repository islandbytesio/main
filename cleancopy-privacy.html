Clean Copy Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 20, 2026

Clean Copy does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

How It Works

The extension processes selected text or table content locally within the user's browser solely for the purpose of converting it into structured formats such as CSV, JSON, TSV, or SQL. No webpage content, browsing history, or personal information is sent to external servers.

Local Processing

All core functionality runs locally in the browser.

Payment Information

For users who purchase Pro access, payment processing and subscription management are handled securely by ExtensionPay. Clean Copy does not access or store payment information.

Contact

If you have questions, contact: support@islandbytes.io
